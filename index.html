About us

Masters of Metal, Innovators in Fabrication

Founded in 2012, SK Engineering works was born out of a deep passion for precision and craftsmanship in sheet metal fabrication. Over the years, we have evolved into a trusted name in the industry, delivering high-quality fabricated solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients. With a commitment to excellence, innovation, and precision, we specialize in transforming raw materials into reliable, custom-engineered products. From intricate designs to large-scale production, our team combines advanced technologies
with unmatched expertise to ensure every project is completed to the highest standards.

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Custom steel metal fabrication involves the
process of designing, cutting, forming, and
assembling steel into specific components or
structures based on client requirements. This
process allows for the creation of intricate
designs, functional parts, and durable products
that cater to a wide range of industries. From
laser cutting and CNC machining to welding and
finishing, every step is tailored to ensure
precision and quality

Stainless Steel Fabrication

We pride ourselves on being a trusted leader in stainless steel fabrication, delivering high-quality, durable, and precision-engineered solutions to meet diverse industry needs. Stainless steel is a versatile material known for its strength, corrosion resistance, and aesthetic appeal, making it ideal for a wide range of applications. With our expertise, we transform raw stainless steel into innovative products that exceed expectations.

M. S Sheet Metal Fabrication

We specialize in M sheet metal fabrication, offering high-quality, durable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to your specific needs. Mild steel is a versatile material widely used in various industries due to its strength, malleability, and affordability. With our advanced fabrication techniques and skilled team, we transform MS sheets into precision-engineered components and structures that meet the highest standards of quality and functionality.
